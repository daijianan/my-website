Musical Composition Toy

This music toy provides a fun way to compose music for children and music lovers, making music composition easier, so that everyone can enjoy the creation of music.

Children may use it as a toy by randomly placing the sound pole on the baseboard, according to their favourite visual choice (such as the city skyline), then the APP can convert it into music. The baseboard of the instrument is connected to the mobile APP, where the melody and rhythm can be optimized. And then you can play the better music.
